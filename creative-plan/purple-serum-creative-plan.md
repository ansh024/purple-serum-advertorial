# Purple Serum Creative Plan

## Creative System

- Visual direction: editorial health-and-beauty article, white base, black typography, restrained lilac accents, natural skin texture, daylight realism, minimal props.
- Use AI generation only for supportive lifestyle scenes and educational graphics.
- Use brand-sourced Perfora photography for any section where accurate packaging, label details, or product-result claims matter.
- Use motion only where it reduces cognitive load: science, usage flow, and stain explanation.
- Casting rule: if any face appears, it must be Indian. Keep styling relatable and contemporary, not luxury-campaign polished.

## Parallel Production Lanes

### Lane A: Brand-Sourced Assets
- Perfora packshot for hero/product reveal
- Approved before/after imagery
- Review screenshots or licensed UGC
- Pump-dispense macro or bottle texture close-up

### Lane B: AI Lifestyle Assets
- Mirror-after-coffee scene with Indian woman
- Before-meeting front-camera scene with Indian professional
- Optional wedding-season or event-prep lifestyle frame with Indian talent

### Lane C: Design-Native Graphics
- Tooth anatomy explainer
- Whitening comparison table
- Ingredient board
- Pull-quote cards and callout boxes

### Lane D: Motion / GIF Assets
- Stain buildup explainer
- Purple neutralises yellow explainer
- How-to-use loop

## Face And Talent Direction

- Prioritise Indian faces with believable skin texture and expressions.
- Avoid western stock-photo styling if talent appears anywhere in the article.
- Wardrobe should feel urban Indian and current: smart casual, wedding guest, workwear, or at-home basics.
- Keep settings locally believable: apartment mirrors, office corridors, cafe tables, vanity counters, wedding dressing areas.
- Expression goal: mild self-awareness, relief, and confidence. Avoid exaggerated smile-ad poses.
